New game announcement

16th may 2022

The game will have an online leaderboard hosting the top 12 scores in game as well as a leaderboard on the website. This is the first game i am really proud of and I can't wait to share it with you guys. I will create a itch.io page for the game soon and will release screenshots soon, I will also continue to update the game for the following months after the games release and will continue to fix and add new features. Thats all for now hope you are looking forward to it - Theo
