About us

Lufton co is an organisation that aims to create safe faciliys for south somerset. We strive for inclucivity and to create a safe space for our community. We offer a safe space for anybody to book and use for events ranging from partys to formal events. We also hold many different clubs such as debate club, LGBTQ+ awareness club and a mental health awareness club who host our mental health day events.

Our goals

  • Create a safe space for our community
  • Have easy to access support and help
  • Allow everybody in our community to feel welcome
  • and have fun!
