About us

Lufton co was created as a way to bring our community together and to raise money for various seasonal events. Our previous events have raised a total of 400K for the referbishment of the community hall and a brand new car park. We also host soup kitchens for those in need on monday, wednesday, friday and sunday and for christmas we convert the main hall into a homeless shelter for two weeks.
