Introducing: VestaMessages

7th may 2022

I have created a new web app/ app for android called VestaMessages designed after the product VestaBoard. Using VestaMessages you can create VestaBoard style messages and share them with friends. To send a message to a friend you can create your message on the app and then click export, clicking export will generate a string that you can copy and send to friends. All your friends need to do is paste the encoded string into the decoder box and click decode and it will play the message. Currently there isn't any support for emojis or coloured text although i have plans to try to add support in the future. You can access the app from the link below or by going to the games/software section of the website. Thats all for now, happy messaging - Theo
